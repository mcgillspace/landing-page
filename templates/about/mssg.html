Who we are

The space industry is on an up and up since companies like SpaceX have brought innovation and creativity to it. We believe that initiatives taken towards advancing the space industry are necessary for the future of the human race. This belief is the foundation of the McGill Space Systems Group.

It is our intention that by contributing in the research and development of space ready technology, McGill will have a great impact on the industry as a whole. It is, however, important for this group to ensure that Undergraduate students learn about this sector and involve themselves in its development.

In order to accomplish this, the McGill Space Systems Group participates in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC), which involves designing, fabricating, testing and potentially launching a nanosatellite.

Our Mission

One of the founding values of the MSSG is the belief that a comprehensive approach needs to be taken when furthering human activity in space. For this reason the MSSG is a multi-disciplinary group that is comprised of Technical Research, Design Teams (CSDC, SEDS), Education, Conferences and Policy Research (SPIRG). With these different facets, the MSSG acts as a key contributor in the Canadian space sector.
