About Us

The space industry is on an up and up since companies like SpaceX have brought innovation and creativity to it. We believe that initiatives taken towards advancing the space industry are necessary for the future of the human race. This belief is the foundation of the McGill Space Systems Group.

It is our intention that by contributing in the research and development of space ready technology, McGill will have a great impact on the industry as a whole. It is, however, important for this group to ensure that Undergraduate students learn about this sector and involve themselves in its development.

In order to accomplish this, the McGill Space Systems Group participates in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC), which involves designing, fabricating, testing and potentially launching a nanosatellite.

CSDC Team

The MSSG believes that one of the most adequate methods of learning engineering is by working on projects thereby learning the design process in a team. The CSDC group provides the opportunity for students to learn through designing and building. By competing in the CSDC, students gain experience in many engineering disciplines, they will learn how to work in a team and will gain various soft skills.

Education Team

Our team is composed of several undergraduate students coming from various backgrounds, including not only education majors, but also physics, computer science and engineering majors. Having such a diverse group of devoted students, that hold education very close to their hearts proves that this division will be able to spark the scientific interest in many young students, ensuring a bright future for the world of space.
