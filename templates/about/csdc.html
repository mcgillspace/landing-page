CSDC

Design, build, test and launch a 3U CubeSat in lower earth orbit with a star tracker payload to participate in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC).

Who we are

The MSSG believes that one of the most adequate methods of learning engineering is by working on projects thereby learning the design process in a team. The CSDC group provides the opportunity for students to learn through designing and building. By competing in the CSDC, students gain experience in many engineering disciplines, they will learn how to work in a team and will gain various soft skills.

Our Mission

The MSSG CSDC Team works to design, build, test and potentially launch a 3U CubeSat in lower earth orbit with a star tracker payload, and participate in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge.
